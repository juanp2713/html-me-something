IT Service Desk Analyst




My IT Professional career began in 2017, when I was hired on as a Service Desk Analyst for Build A Bear Workshop. I started off just taking calls and working tickets in a system we call 'Heat', where we would find incidents or Service Requests submitted by employees at our stores, warehouses, European and North American corporate offices. This is what we call a Front Line Analyst.


After 8 months, I moved to a secondary role as a Service Desk Analyst called Support. This role takes a step away from just taking calls and working tickets and handles more Desktop Support related issues. I also work extensivley in Active Directory, Exchange and Office 365, imaging PCs and Macs, as well as creating scripts using powershell.
